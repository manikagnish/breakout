Breakout!

How To Play:

Use your right and left arrow keys to move the paddle to bounce the ball up and break the blocks.

You can choose different levels of difficulty from beginner to god. With each level, the speed of the ball will increase.

You will get 3 balls/chances to break all the blocks. If you miss hitting the ball off the paddle three times you lose the game.
